a little bit about me

Hi, I'm Laurynn

Welcome to my studio! I'm a nail tech based in Temple, TX, specializing in custom acrylic and gel-x sets, hand-painted nail art, and clean, long-wearing finishes. Every set is built around what you bring me — a Pinterest board, a color mood, or just a vibe.

My studio is private and by appointment only. Deposits keep my chair reserved just for you, and I always do my best to make each visit relaxing, on time, and glitter-optional (but encouraged).
